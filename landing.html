About Me

Hello! My name is Gwyneth Raquepo but most people call me Gwen. As someone who grew up in the era of computers, I became passionate in technologies. I was also inspired by the works and projects of my engineer dad. Seeing the buildings he worked on come to life introduced me to the idea of creating things. I began to appreciate the process of making something that people can use to improve their daily lives.

I am currently working for the State of Hawaii, primarily programming websites to modernize systems, streamline workflows, and improve the efficiency of state employees. I program in PHP, HTML, CSS, Javascript, and use HeidiSQL for our database. My secondary role is to help clients with tech related issues, setting up computers, and troubleshooting networks.

I was born in the Philippines and I moved to Hawaii when I was eigth years old. I graduated in May of 2023 and I have a Bachelor's Degree in Computer Engineering. During my free time, I go to the gym, hang out with my friends, play computer games, or go for walks. Lastly, you will probably never see me go on with my day without a cup of Matcha.
